The Creator

Just a guy who's tired of social media but still wants to stay informed

The Concept

Three of the world's most trending stories, delivered every day

The Goal

Maybe one day, instead of conflict and disappointment, we'll see more of the good things

The Process

Each poster is fully automated, including the design. I use open data sources and libraries to generate a unique pattern every day. The larger the circle, the more significant the news

Data Sources

This project is built using open-source tools and libraries. I use p5.js for generative visualization, Node.js for the automated news-gathering bot, and NewsAPI to fetch the most significant global news every day.
